About GDGFEST

About GDG FEST '25 – SEASON 2 🎉


GDG FEST '25 – Season 2 is an international-level tech fest organized by Google Developer Groups On Campus, KSR Institute of Engineering and Technology. This one-day tech extravaganza, happening on March 21, is designed to bring together tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators for an action-packed experience filled with learning, collaboration, and competition.

About GDG KSRIET


GDG KSRIET is a student-led chapter fostering innovation and collaboration as part of the Google Developer Groups (GDG) network.

We organized Namakkal’s first 24-hour hackathon in 2023, with 500+ participants, followed by a bigger 2024 edition with 800+ registrations and 60 offline teams.

Beyond hackathons, we hosted GDSC Fest, an Indo-Malaysian Hackathon, and saw 10+ teams advance in the Google Solution Challenge.

Committed to hands-on learning and industry connections, we continue to empower the next generation of tech leaders.
